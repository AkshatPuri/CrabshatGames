Trip: Endless Runner

About the game!

Trip is a game about the trip of a creature into two different worlds where he has to dodge the enemies and choose every move ,the enemies in Trip are sometimes not visible,player has to check for the enemies lane every time and accordingly make his move.Most addictive game,with optimized performance made with unity,its kind of an endless runner ,join the trip of this creature into two different worlds.

Hunt for the high score , lot of unlockables characters are available. Funky looking water creatures. Get ready for this trip into two different color changing worlds.

Features:

  • -Challenge yourself to hunt for high score in every trip.
  • -Vibration controls for more effectiveness.
  • -Tap the Up and Down button to change characters direction.
  • -Swipe on the screen for knowing where the object is in the different worlds.

Category: Casual
